# Disclaimer / 免責聲明

## English

The mock prompts and articles in this repository are intended for **defensive security research and education**. They are designed to be used against LLM systems that you:

1. Own (e.g., self-hosted Ollama instances, internal company LLMs)
2. Are explicitly authorized to test (e.g., via written agreement with the operator)

**Do not** use these prompts to repeatedly stress-test third-party LLM services without authorization. Doing so may violate the Terms of Service of those providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.) and could result in account suspension.

This repository aligns with the principles of [OWASP LLM Top 10 (2025)](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-10-for-large-language-model-applications/), specifically LLM01: Prompt Injection. The samples here are documentation-style references, not weaponized payloads.

The author and contributors assume no liability for misuse of this material.
